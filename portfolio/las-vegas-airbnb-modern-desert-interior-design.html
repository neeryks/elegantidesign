Las Vegas Airbnb - Fun Modern Desert Glam Design

A Stylish, Guest-Ready Las Vegas Vacation Home Designed for Comfort & Play

Project Overview

  • Location: Las Vegas, Nevada

  • Layout: 2,000 sq. ft. interior + 1,000 sq. ft. exterior

  • Type: Airbnb Vacation Home

  • Style: Modern Desert Glam with Vegas-Inspired Color Stories

  • Scope: Full 4 bedroom / 2 baths house + remodeling

  • Services: Concept Development, Full Kitchen Renovation, Flooring Replacement, 2 Bathroom Remodels, Outdoor Living Concept, Furniture & Decor Sourcing (Shopping list)

  • Furnishing Budget: $30,000

Design Approach

For this Las Vegas Airbnb, the vision was to capture the spirit of Vegas—playful, bold, and full of personality, without crossing into kitsch. We crafted a home that feels elevated and modern, designed for groups up to eight, and layered with subtle nods to iconic resorts and desert landscapes.

The heart of the project was a full renovation: a new kitchen, reimagined bathrooms, updated flooring, and a fully planned outdoor retreat. Inside, color palettes mimic the mood of famous Vegas hotels—emerald greens, sunset pinks, deep sapphire blues—translated into a warm, contemporary interior. The design balances vivid moments with grounding neutrals, creating spaces that feel both photogenic and relaxing after a day on the Strip.

Clean lines, highly functional layouts, durable surfaces, and thoughtful host-friendly touches make the home both beautiful and optimized for short-term guests. Every room carries a unique identity while maintaining a cohesive flow through shared textures, materials, and a distinctly modern desert sensibility.

Key Features

  • Brand-new kitchen with sage-green cabinetry, modern herringbone backsplash, quartz countertops, and matte black hardware

  • Open-concept living + dining designed for groups, featuring a generously sized sectional and sculptural lighting

  • Vegas-inspired accent walls that echo vintage marquees, desert sunsets, and resort-style motifs

  • Textured wallpapers and bold paint choices including deep greens, terracotta, and jewel-toned blues

  • Bedroom “themes” inspired by muted versions of iconic Vegas hotels — from desert oasis greens to Bellagio-style golds to Cosmopolitan blues to Flamingo pinks

  • Fully remodeled bathrooms with clean tilework, contemporary fixtures, and serene color palettes

Color Scheme

A modern desert-meets-Vegas palette:

  • Sage + deep emerald greens (kitchen and primary bedroom)

  • Warm terracotta + blush tones (sunset-inspired guest room)

  • Sapphire and geometric blues (third bedroom inspired by boutique hotel suites)

  • Earthy neutrals in flooring, living room seating, and drapery

  • Pops of orange that echo poolside cabanas and desert sunsets

  • Black accents for grounding and modern contrast

Interior Design Services - Concept to Completion.

Whether you're redesigning a single room or starting from scratch, our virtual process makes interior design effortless and elevated. We guide you from layout and concept to photorealistic 3D renderings and curated shopping lists—so you can shop, style, and settle in with confidence.

Concept and Moodboard

We start with a strong vision. Your inspiration, lifestyle, and goals guide our curated moodboards—defining your space's tone before any design begins.

Floor Plan and 3D Layouts

We turn your space into a thoughtful plan. Our custom 2D and 3D layouts show furniture placement, flow, and proportions—so you can visualize it all before moving forward.

Photo Realistic Rendering

From concept to lifelike visuals. Our photorealistic renderings help you see every detail—furniture, finishes, lighting—so you can make decisions with clarity and confidence.

Shopping List

Skip the guesswork. We deliver a complete, clickable shopping list of furnishings and décor—sourced to match your style, budget, and timeline.

Júlia de Miranda.

Founder and Chief Designer

    Júlia de Miranda is a Brazilian-born Architect and Interior Designer with over six years of experience transforming spaces across the U.S., Europe, and South America.

    As the founder of Eleganti Design, she brings a globally inspired, client-focused approach to every project—balancing style, function, and emotional resonance.


  From New York brownstones and Paris rentals to Brazilian nurseries and boutique retail spaces, Júlia’s work blends soft modernism with subtle storytelling. Known for her warm, intuitive process, she helps clients design spaces that feel both elevated and personal—no matter where they’re located.
